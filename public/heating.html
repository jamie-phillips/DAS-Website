DAS Plumbing & Heating specialise in central heating installation & repairs.

If your central heating system is in need of repair, your boiler needs servicing or your radiators are temperamental, then give me a call for a free no obligation quote. There is no job too small or too big, from replacing a valve or repairing a leaky pipe to a complete new installation. All of our boilers are energy efficient and could save you at least 30% of your annual gas bill!

Landlord Gas Safety Certificates

Gas Safe registered engineers will complete your annual inspection and provide you with the Landlord Gas Safety Certificate.

Natural Gas & LPG specialists

LPG or liquid petroleum gas is ideal for properties with no mains gas supply. I am a gas safe registered LPG engineer and will ensure that your installation, service or repair runs smoothly.

I offer a wide range of LPG products and services. From repairs and boiler servicing to a full LPG heating system installation.
